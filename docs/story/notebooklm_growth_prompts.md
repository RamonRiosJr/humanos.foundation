This document contains high-fidelity prompts and narrative architectures designed for use with NotebookLM and AI video generators. These prompts focus on the **Humanos Movement**, the **Sovereign Library**, and the **Patient Advocacy** mission of the Foundation.

Use these to generate emotionally resonant, brand-aligned content that explains the "Why" behind the movement and the "How" of the technology.

### 🛡️ Global Strategic Guardrails & Content Restrictions (MANDATORY)

**To prevent intellectual property leaks and maintain project stealth, every generated output MUST adhere to these restrictions:**

1.  **Anti-Engineeer Protocol:** Do NOT explain the specific technical "plumbing." Focus 100% on the **User Experience (UX)** and the **Patient Outcome**.
2.  **The "Black Box" Handshake:** When discussing the QR bridge or data transfer, use terms like "Secure Mathematical Handshake" or "Proprietary Sovereign Sync." Never mention the specific cryptographic algorithms, key rotation periods, or multi-sig logic.
3.  **No Backend Disclosure:** Never mention "Odoo," "REST APIs," or specific server-side architectures. To the world, hOS is a "Unified Local Layer" that works like magic.
4.  **Data Structure Stealth:** Do not describe how the data is stored on disk (e.g., JSON schemas, blobs, or relational maps). Use metaphors like "Encrypted Sovereignty Vault" or "Immutable Life-Graph."
5.  **Stealth Branding:** Never reference "Aura" or "Aura hOS" in these videos. Use only "Humanos Foundation" or "patient-sovereign hOS."
